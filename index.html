03 — The Process

How It Works

Step 1
Source
Off-market deal sourcing through direct owner outreach, AI-powered market scanning, and broker network. We target dated properties in premium locations with clear renovation upside.
Step 2
Buy
Property purchased in investor's name (title deed). Acquisition costs, DLD fees, and agency commission factored into the deal budget. Property title provides full asset protection.
Step 3
Renovate
Desert Build manages construction with fixed-price contracts, milestone payments, and compressed contractor margins. Design-led renovations targeting current buyer expectations.
Step 4
Sell
Haysal Real Estate handles the exit — professional photography, staging, multi-platform marketing. Targeting premium buyers willing to pay for turnkey quality.
Step 5
Profit
Net profit split 70/30 — 70% to the investor, 30% to the operator. Clean, transparent, and aligned incentives.

12 — Risk Management

Risk & Safety Nets

Title Protection
Property title deed in investor's name on all flip deals. Your asset, your security.
Rental Fallback
If exit takes longer, pivot to rental income at 5–15% gross yield depending on tier and location.
Fixed-Price Contracts
Desert Build provides fixed-price renovation contracts with milestone payments — no cost overrun surprises.
Escrow Protection
Development projects use RERA escrow accounts. Flip deals use milestone-based fund release at higher tiers.
Compressed Margins
Desert Build's volume relationships save 15–20% on construction vs. third-party contractors.
Market Intelligence
AI-powered deal scoring and comparable analysis ensures we don't overpay. Data before emotion.
